Review: Countdown to the 2028 Presidential Election

As the 2028 U.S. Presidential Election draws closer, the political landscape remains volatile and unpredictable. With a divided electorate and numerous challenges, the stakes for this election are unprecedented. The future of the nation hinges on decisions that will shape the direction of American democracy, with critical issues at play regarding governance, the economy, and foreign relations.

Media Influence and the Role of Social Media

In recent years, social media has become a double-edged sword in political engagement. While it has democratized information and empowered ordinary citizens, it has also been weaponized in authoritarian regimes to manipulate public opinion and suppress dissent. The 2028 election will undoubtedly see more of this dynamic play out, with social media platforms being utilized to influence voter behavior, particularly among younger demographics. As noted by Yilmaz et al. (2024), platforms like Twitter and Facebook are crucial in shaping political discourse in democracies and authoritarian states alike.

"Social media remains both a powerful tool for political engagement and a platform for authoritarian regimes to influence public perception." - Political Analyst

The Impact of Government Oversight on Public Agencies

Another critical issue is the government's increasing oversight over scientific and public health institutions. The Trump administration's restrictions on the CDC's ability to freely communicate research findings have raised concerns about the manipulation of scientific data for political gain. As Huang and Stone (2025) reported, these actions have led to severe delays in the release of vital public health information, which could impact voters' trust in the electoral process and the government's handling of national crises.
